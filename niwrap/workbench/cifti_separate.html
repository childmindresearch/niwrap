335    """
336    cifti-separate by Washington University School of Medicin.
337    
338    Write a cifti structure as metric, label or volume.
339    
340    For dtseries, dscalar, and dlabel, use COLUMN for <direction>, and if you
341    have a symmetric dconn, COLUMN is more efficient.
342    
343    You must specify at least one of -metric, -volume-all, -volume, or -label
344    for this command to do anything. Output volumes will spatially line up with

373    HIPPOCAMPUS_RIGHT

384    
385    Args:
386        cifti_in: the cifti to separate a component of.
387        direction: which direction to separate into components, ROW or COLUMN.
388        volume_all: separate all volume structures into a volume file.
389        label: separate a surface model into a surface label file.
390        metric: separate a surface model into a metric file.
391        volume: separate a volume structure into a volume file.
